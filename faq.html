FAQ

How long do custom orders take?

Timeframes for custom orders vary depending on size, but generally start at 4 weeks. For a timeframe on a specific item, please feel free to reach out to us!

Where do you ship to?

We currently only ship to the US & Canada, but are hoping to expand soon!

What is your return policy?

Due to the custom nature of our items, we do not accept returns outside of damaged items due to postage.

How do I take care of my plushie?

We recommend cleaning plushies with a warm, damp cloth. Due to their handmade nature, we recommend avoiding harsh handling/tugging on the features.
